# Homepage Blueprint

> Project Harvest Homepage V2 Blueprint  
> Version: 1.0

---

# 1. Homepage Goal

The homepage should help global buyers understand Harvest within 30 seconds:

- What we supply
- Why Harvest is reliable
- How our supply chain works
- Which markets we serve
- How to request a quote

---

# 2. Core Brand Message

From Our Fields to Global Markets.

Harvest provides fresh vegetables, fresh fruits, frozen foods and prepared food solutions through self-owned planting bases, factory-direct processing, self-owned cold chain warehousing and full product traceability.

# 4. Image Direction

The homepage should prioritize real business photos:

- Planting bases
- Factory processing
- Cold chain warehouse
- Product packing
- Container loading
- Export products

Avoid cartoon or unrealistic AI-style images where possible.

---

# 5. Communication Principles

The homepage should avoid exaggerated claims.

Use factual, specific language:

Instead of:

Best supplier

Use:

Self-owned planting bases and full product traceability

Instead of:

High quality

Use:

Quality managed from planting to export

Instead of:

Professional service

Use:

Customized packaging, private label and OEM support
