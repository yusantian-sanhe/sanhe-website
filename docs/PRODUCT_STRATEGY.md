# 1. Company Positioning

Harvest is a China-based supplier specializing in the export of fresh vegetables, fresh fruits, frozen foods and prepared foods.

We provide integrated supply solutions for global importers, wholesalers, distributors, supermarkets, food manufacturers and food service companies.

Our goal is not only to export products, but also to become a long-term supply chain partner for our customers.

# 6. Website Goal

The website should present Harvest as a professional global supplier instead of a simple trading company.

Every page should help international buyers quickly understand:

- What products we supply
- Why customers choose Harvest
- Which markets we serve
- How we control quality
- How to contact us
