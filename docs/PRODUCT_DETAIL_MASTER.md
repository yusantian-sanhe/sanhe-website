# SanHe Product Detail Master

Version: 1.0

Status: Production Standard

Last Updated: 2026-07

---

# Purpose

This document defines the standard structure for every SanHe product detail page.

Its purpose is to ensure that every product page:

- Builds buyer trust
- Demonstrates SanHe’s supply capability
- Answers buyer questions
- Encourages quotation requests
- Supports multilingual consistency
- Can be reused across all products

Every product page should follow this structure unless there is a clear business reason to do otherwise.

---

# Product Philosophy

SanHe does not simply sell products.

SanHe provides reliable international food supply chain solutions.

Products demonstrate capability.

Capability builds trust.

Trust leads to cooperation.

# Standard Page Structure

Every product page consists of the following sections.

## Section 1

Purpose

Content

• Product Subtitle

• Product Image

• Trust Badges

Primary CTA

Request a Quote

Secondary CTA

Explore Products

---

## Section 2

Why Buyers Choose SanHe

Purpose

Explain why buyers should cooperate with SanHe instead of another supplier.

Recommended cards

• Own Planting Bases

• Stable Supply

• Strict Quality Control

• Export Experience

• Flexible Packaging

• Cold Chain Logistics

---

## Section 3

Product Overview

Purpose

Introduce the product.

Recommended length

100–200 words.

Should include

• Product description

• Main characteristics

• Typical export markets

• Typical applications

Avoid

Long encyclopedia-style introductions.

---

## Section 4

Purpose

Show where the product is commonly used.

• Wholesale

• Food Processing

• Supermarkets

• Food Service

# Writing Principles

Write for professional buyers.

Not consumers.

Use:

Short paragraphs.

Business language.

Facts.

Clear specifications.

Avoid:

Marketing slogans.

Long introductions.

Emotional language.

Unverified claims.
