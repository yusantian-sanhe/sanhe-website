# SanHe Content Guidelines

Version: 1.0

Status: Production Standard

Last Updated: 2026-07

---

# Purpose

This document defines the writing standards for all SanHe website content.

It ensures consistency across:

- Homepage
- Products
- Product Details
- Markets
- Quality
- About
- Contact
- CMS
- Future Blog & News
- Multilingual translations

---

# Writing Objective

Every piece of content should help buyers:

- Understand SanHe
- Trust SanHe
- Understand the product
- Reduce purchasing uncertainty
- Start cooperation

The purpose is not to impress.

The purpose is to help buyers make business decisions.

---

# Target Audience

Primary audience:

- Food Importers
- Food Wholesalers
- Food Manufacturers
- Supermarket Buyers
- Brand Owners
- Purchasing Managers

Never write for consumers.

---

# Writing Style

Content should be:

Professional

Clear

Reliable

Concise

International

Buyer-focused

---

# Paragraph Length

Recommended:

2–4 sentences

Avoid very long paragraphs.

---

# Sentence Length

Prefer:

10–25 words

Avoid unnecessary complexity.

---

# Headlines

Headlines should answer buyer questions.

Good examples:

Why Buyers Choose SanHe

Stable Export Supply

Professional Quality Control

Flexible Packaging Solutions

Request a Quote

Avoid:

Welcome

Our Story

Amazing Products

Best Supplier

---

# Product Descriptions

Describe:

- Product
- Characteristics
- Applications
- Export advantages
- Packaging
- Cooperation

Avoid:

History

Scientific encyclopedia content

Marketing exaggeration

---

# Business Language

Prefer:

Reliable Supply

Export-ready

Quality Inspection

Cold Chain

OEM Service

Traceability

Customized Packaging

Food Safety

International Logistics

Avoid:

Best Quality

Perfect Product

Cheapest Price

World's Number One

Guaranteed Success

---

# Numbers

Only publish numbers that have been verified.

Examples:

Years of experience

Export countries

Storage capacity

Container loading

Production capacity

Never invent numbers.

---

# Tone

Professional

Helpful

Transparent

Confident

Never sound:

Aggressive

Pushy

Over-promising

---

# Calls to Action

Primary:

Request a Quote

Secondary:

Explore Products

Do not use multiple competing CTA buttons.

---

# Image Captions

Captions should explain business value.

Example:

Fresh Ginger prepared for export under strict quality inspection.

Avoid:

Beautiful ginger.

---

# SEO Writing

Each page should have:

Unique H1

Unique Title

Unique Description

Relevant Keywords

Internal Links

Natural wording

Avoid keyword stuffing.

---

# Translation Rules

English is the master language.

Translations should preserve:

Meaning

Tone

Business terminology

CTA consistency

Do not translate word-for-word if a more natural professional expression exists.

---

# Approved Terminology

Use consistently:

Fresh Vegetables

Fresh Fruits

Frozen Foods

Prepared Foods

Cold Chain

Traceability

Private Label

OEM

MOQ

Lead Time

Export Market

Food Safety

Quality Assurance

---

# Words to Avoid

Best

Perfect

Cheapest

Unlimited

100% Guaranteed

No Risk

Number One

Use factual alternatives instead.

---

# Buyer-first Rule

Before publishing any content, ask:

Does this answer a buyer's question?

If not,

rewrite it.

---

# Final Principle

Good content is not written to impress search engines.

Good content helps buyers make confident business decisions.
